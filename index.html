About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineer with a Masters in Psychology who currently earns a living as a Project Controls Manger in Oil and Gas. Due to a volitile energy market, and an overwhelming desire to live full-time in Golden BC; I decided it was time to become a FULL-STACK WEB DEVELOPER.


In February 2016, I became a student at Thinkful (while keeping my day job) and began learning HTML5 and CSS3. As of this writing on March 11, 2016 I am just about to submit my first portfolio project and head into the workd of jQuery, JavaScript, and AJAX. Currently, I work in Calgary, AB three days a week, and the rest of the time I am where I love to be. I make the commute back-and-forth between Calgary and Golden with my husband Todd and our feisty dog Rusty. I love to ski, bike, surf, read, and be outside as much as possible.
